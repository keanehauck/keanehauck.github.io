Lovely writeup of some of the most frequent pitfalls and lapses of judgment to which people can be victims when encountering statistics. 

My favorite part is that it is geared towards an audience with no formal statistical training. After reading the book, you feel equipped to tackle statistical misinformation with just your own powers of logic and deduction.
