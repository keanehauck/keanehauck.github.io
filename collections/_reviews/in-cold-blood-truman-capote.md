In Cold Blood was an amazing novel. I was inspired to read it after finishing *The Devil in the White City* by Erik Larson. Unfortunately, I may have carried too many expectations from Larson's novel into my experience with this one. I was actually expecting more history and less murder-drama. Still, I highly recommend this book.
